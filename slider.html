Responsive. Modular: can be more than one on the page. Defaults to 100% container width, 500px height. Pauses slideshow on hover.

Images are Background-images. Filling the container div regardless of the image size.

Behavior you can change:
  • autoPlay: "off"
  • showArrows: "off"
  • hideDots: "on"
  • hoverZoom: "off"
  • titleBarTop: "on"
  • Change slide pauses and fade speeds
