Mike is an award winning Self-Shooting Director, Editor and fully qualified Drone Pilot, working extensively in broadcast and commercial video production.

He has over 15 years experience in the industry and specialises in high-end observational documentary, presenter led, blue lights, children's television and short drama.

Originally from Barry Island, Mike is dedicated to sharing Welsh stories with global audiences through the relm of both factual and drama.

Mike is very friendly and approachable and the proud father of two beautiful, crazy little girls.
